About the RTA

An acronym for setting goals you will be able to follow and achieve. The acronym SMART stands for:

(Click on each word for more information)

SPECIFIC

Keep the goal as specific as possible so you know exactly what you want to achieve. What? How? Where?

MEASUREABLE

Keep the goal as specific as possible so you know exactly what you want to achieve. What? How? Where?

ATTAINABLE

Goals show push you, but still need to be completed. Are your goals achievable?

RELEVANT

Is the goal and timeframe realistic for you to complete?

TIME-BASED

Do you have a timeframe listed? This helps for motivation in completing that goal as well as measuring how well you are going in being able to actually achieve your goal.

Broad Goal: I want to start a business.

Specific: I will sell handmade cards through Etsy.com.
Measurable: I will be ready to take my first Etsy order within four weeks, and I will aim to sell a minimum of five cards per week.
Attainable: I will get set up on Etsy first. Then, I will build an inventory of 30 handmade cards to sell. Finally, I will promote my business and build customer relationships through word of mouth, referrals and local networking.
Relevant: Selling handmade cards will allow me to benefit financially from my favourite hobby.
Time-Based: My Etsy store will be up and running within four weeks, and I will have an inventory of 30 cards to sell within six weeks.

SMART Goal: Within a month, I am going to get set up to sell handmade cards on Etsy, which will allow me to benefit financially from my favourite hobby.
